Our Story

Four Decades of
Engineering Excellence

"From a small workshop in Faridabad to a trusted partner of India's largest OEMs — our journey is built on precision."

KBI was established in 1981 in Faridabad, Haryana as Kochar Agro Industries, immediately associating with Maruti Suzuki India Limited. Over four decades we have grown into a key manufacturer of Sheet Metal & Fabrication components for the automobile industry.

A second unit in Alwar, Rajasthan manufactures power and telecom cable inputs — galvanized steel tape, strips, and wire — serving infrastructure and utilities sectors nationwide.

Our Journey

Milestones That
Shaped KBI

1981

Established

As Kochar Agro Industries, got associated with Maruti Suzuki India Limited (MSIL).

1995

Major Milestone

Associated with VECV (VE Commercial Vehicles) and Escorts Kubota Limited.

2005

Takeover

The Khandelwal family acquired the company, ushering in a new era of growth.

2007

Growth

Associated with CNH Industrial — global leader in agricultural and construction equipment.

2008

Expansion

Started manufacturing galvanized strips and steel tapes in a new unit at Alwar, Rajasthan.

2022

Bold Expansion

Associated with Kobelco India Limited — leading excavator manufacturer.

2023

Rising Growth

Associated with Mahindra Defence Systems — diversifying into defence manufacturing.

2024

Bracing for the Future

Associated with Euler Motors — entering the electric vehicle components segment.
