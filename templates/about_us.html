Our Story

KBI got established in 1981 in Faridabad,Haryana. It is a key manufacturer of Sheet metal & Fabrication components for automobile industry. Another Unit of the Company manufactures power and telecom cable inputs in Alwar,Rajasthan.

Today, we stand as a testament to dedication, growth, and the relentless pursuit of perfection. Every challenge we've faced has strengthened our resolve to deliver exceptional value to our clients and partners.

Amit Kapoor

Chief Executive Officer (Autocomponents)

Mr. Amit Kapoor, is a Mechanical Engineering Graduate with MBA in Operations Management. He is a result oriented professional with over 26 years of work experience in Business Development, Green Field Projects, Plant Operations and has a diverse professional background and expertise in the fields of Construction / Material Handling Equipment’s, Cement and Mining Industry, Pre Engineered Buildings and Heavy Steel Fabrication for Railway Bridges, Flyovers, Metro Stations and Power Plant Structures. Having contributed significantly to companies like Action Construction Equipment Limited, FLSmidth Private Limited, Kirby Building Systems India Limited's Lloyds Group, he possesses a wealth of experience in the industry and is known for his strategic leadership. Mr. Kapoor has played an instrumental role in the success of these renowned companies. His career journey showcases a commitment to excellence, innovative problem-solving, and effective team management. With a focus on construction equipment, building systems, and broader industrial solutions, he has left a lasting impact on each organization he has been associated with. Mr. Amit Kapoor's career reflects a dedication to driving growth and achieving results in the dynamic sectors he has been a part of.
