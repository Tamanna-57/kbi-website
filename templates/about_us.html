Our Story

Our Story

Built on
Precision &
Partnership

KBI was founded in 1981 in Faridabad, Haryana as Kochar Agro Industries — immediately partnering with Maruti Suzuki India Limited. Over four decades, we have grown into a reliable manufacturer of sheet metal and fabrication components for the automobile industry.

Our second plant in Alwar, Rajasthan produces galvanized steel tape, wire, and strip for power and telecom cable applications — serving India's infrastructure backbone.
