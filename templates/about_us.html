Our Journey

Milestones That
Shaped KBI

1981

Established

As Kochar Agro Industries, associated with Maruti Suzuki India Limited (MSIL).

1995

Major Milestone

Associated with VECV (VE Commercial Vehicles) and Escorts Kubota Limited.

2005

Takeover

The Khandelwal family acquired the company, ushering in a new era of growth.

2007

Growth

Associated with CNH Industrial — global leader in agricultural and construction equipment.

2008

Expansion

New unit in Alwar, Rajasthan manufacturing galvanized strips and steel tapes for power & telecom.

2022

Bold Expansion

Associated with Kobelco India Limited — leading excavator manufacturer.

2023

Rising Growth

Associated with Mahindra Defence Systems — diversifying into defence manufacturing.

2024

Future-Ready

Associated with Euler Motors — entering the electric vehicle components segment.
